Breakfast

1.White Bean Salad

Health benifits

Protein : White beans are an excellent plant-based source of protein, which is essential for muscle health and overall body function.
Fiber : Beans are rich in dietary fiber, promoting digestive health and helping to regulate blood sugar levels.
Vitamins and Minerals: The vegetables contribute essential vitamins and minerals, such as vitamin C, potassium, and antioxidants.
Healthy Fats: The olive oil in the dressing provides heart-healthy monounsaturated fats.
Low in Saturated Fat: This salad is naturally low in saturated fat, making it a heart-healthy option.
Remember, you can customize the salad based on your preferences and dietary needs. Enjoy it as a light and nutritious meal or as a side dish with grilled chicken or fish.

A refreshing salad with a mix of colorful vegetables. Perfect for a light Breakfast.
